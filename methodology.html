Methodology & Data Integrity

This document describes the data collection, processing, and publication approach for the LiftWatch competitive intelligence portal.

Data Sources

Job postings are collected from the following public sources:

  • SEEK — www.seek.com.au — Major Australian job board with extensive lift and elevator industry coverage
  • Indeed — www.indeed.com.au — Aggregated job board with significant Australian lift sector presence
  • Jora — www.jora.com — Vertical job aggregator with detailed industry filtering
  • Company Career Pages — Direct career pages for major operators including Otis, Schindler, KONE, Simplex, and regional installers

All data sources are publicly accessible and require no authentication. Data collection respects robots.txt guidelines and terms of service for all platforms.

Collection Process

Jobs are collected through daily automated scanning of job boards and career pages. The process includes:

  1. Automated search queries targeting lift, elevator, and vertical transportation roles across all states
  2. Metadata extraction: title, company, location, salary, job type, source URL, posting date
  3. Deduplication and matching against historical database
  4. PDF archival of original job posting to Google Drive
  5. Manual review and categorization by role type (technician, installer, sales, management, etc.)
  6. Daily data refresh and analytics recalculation

Data Categories & Definitions

Role Type — Primary job function:

  • Technician — Service, maintenance, repair, installation of lift systems
  • Mechanic — Mechanical design, fabrication, or equipment maintenance
  • Electrician — Electrical installation and maintenance for lift systems
  • Sales — Business development, account management, sales engineering
  • Project Manager — Project coordination, site supervision, delivery management
  • Other — Administrative, customer service, finance, HR, or mixed roles

Status — Job posting state:

  • New — Posted or refreshed in the last 7 days
  • Active — Posted more than 7 days ago but still visible on job boards
  • Closed — No longer visible; position likely filled

Update Frequency

The portal is updated daily with new job postings and removed positions. Metadata is current as of the most recent scan. Historical data is preserved in the database to track hiring trends over time.

Known Limitations

  • Job Board Coverage: Data reflects only publicly posted positions. Internal transfers, fill rates, and hiring timelines are not captured.
  • Duplicate Listings: The same position may appear across multiple job boards or be reposted frequently. Deduplication is performed but may not be perfect.
  • Salary Data: Many listings do not include salary information; comparisons should account for missing data.
  • Geographic Precision: Location data reflects stated job location, which may be office-based rather than field work location.
  • Small Operators: Regional and small specialist lift companies may not appear due to limited job board presence.
  • Contractor vs. Employee: The database does not distinguish between permanent and contractor roles except where explicitly stated.

Data Quality & Verification

All data goes through automated validation:

  • Duplicate detection and removal by URL and posting date
  • Company name standardization and fuzzy matching
  • State and location validation against Australian postcodes
  • Salary parsing and range extraction from free-text fields
  • Source URL validation and archival

Manual spot checks are performed weekly. If you identify data errors or outdated listings, please report via the feedback link at the bottom of each page.

Privacy & Data Usage

This portal displays only publicly posted job listings. No personal data is collected, stored, or displayed. All information originates from public job boards and company websites.

Data may be used for competitive analysis, market research, and business intelligence purposes. Redistribution of the dataset is permitted for non-commercial use with attribution to LiftWatch.

Technical Implementation

The portal is a static HTML/CSS/JavaScript application hosted on GitHub Pages. Data is loaded from a single JSON file updated daily. No tracking, cookies, or external analytics are used. All data processing occurs in the user's browser.

Source code and methodology details are available on GitHub: github.com/markevolvere/liftwatch-dashboard
